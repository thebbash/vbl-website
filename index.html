Sustainability

Building for the Climate We Actually Operate In

West Africa is not a temperate climate. Heat intensity, seasonal flooding, and material degradation under tropical conditions are real engineering challenges — and we account for all of them.

We integrate passive cooling strategies, climate-appropriate material specifications, and flood-resilient infrastructure design as standard practice. Sustainability is not a checkbox for us. It is structural intelligence.
